Overview

You’ve read my resume, so I’ll skip that spiel. I’m a person that creates. I come to conclusions through exploration, experimentation and collaboration.


I'm also a person who thinks good people, music, and Minneapolis brew is the recipe for happiness.

My Approach

Explore and Understand

Research is essential to the creative. Understanding isn’t just knowing the project, its knowing the purpose. Defining that is purpose is more important than just making something look pretty.

Understanding what the design is supposed to accomplish helps me communicate effectively with the consumer and instills greater value in the work.

Experiment

I gather inspiration from everything I see and hear–music, friends, photography, food, nature–the list goes on. Everything I interact with can find its way into my work. Working in these different mediums can bring some fire too.

I’ve also found a lot of inspiration by exploring different design media. Experience with photography and animation informs the work I do in Illustrator.

I love being involved in a field that constantly provides new opportunities for growth.

Collaborate

One mind is rarely enough to come up with a game-changing idea. I’m creative and all, but nothing helps me find great solutions better than bouncing ideas off of others, whether they’re designers or not.

That’s why I want to develop my skills at an agency where strategists understand creatives and vice versa. The more people can understand the full process, the easier it is to work together.

The Good Life

Good People

There’s nothing better than a good couch, good tunes and good conversation. I surround myself with people that value connection. They’ve made me a better designer by helping me be more emotionally articulate and understanding of diverse perspectives. They’ve also given me plenty of feedback on my designs over the years

This, of course, has made its way into my design process. I love showing everyone everything, and believe working in every aspect of a project, not just design, will deeply root me in the objective.

Music

I find it incredible that an artist can use the same scales and instruments as another and come up with something completely original. Translating emotion into sound is an awesome form of creativity. I try to channel that creativity in my design process by asking myself how an image can convey the deeper meaning behind a project.

Music has always been a part of my life. I’ve taken a stab at learning multiple instruments, sung since I was a kid and have even participated in a few drum circles.

Brewskis

How is beer related to design? Well let me tell you: from the golden hues to the crafty cans, beer inspires. It can be fruity, dank, floral, bold or even bitter. There is an insane amount of flavors to choose from, yet some brewers manage to find perfect balances in their creations.

A sip sends me straight to my happy place (not to mention the buzz). Just thinking of the effort and precision that goes into the process makes drinking good beer so much more enjoyable.
